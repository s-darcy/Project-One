Shane Darcy's Biography

I am a profoundly blessed husband and father of two boys with a 3rd little one on the way. I live in Fort Mill, SC, a thriving suburban community south of Charlotte, NC.

Born in raised in Lawrenceville, GA, a suburb of Atlanta, I carry an inseparable pride for the Atlanta Braves, Atlanta Falcons, and Georgia Bulldogs.

I have had the privilege to grow professionally alongside and within a fast-paced, ‘write-in-pencil,’ winning culture that makes up Red Ventures for the past seven years. While at RV, I have sold, monitored calls for quality assurance, facilitated contracts, and maintained partner relations.

For the last two years I have immersed myself in code. Most nights (and days if I can get away with it), I plug away for hours completing nearly all the coursework for HTML, CSS, and Javascript found in both Treehouse and Codcademy. I have applied these skills by building a 27 page website and blog. Recently, Red Ventures has rocketed my learning of web development with their ITA Course, which is a 6 month apprenticeship-type program designed to groom me for a backend position in the company.

Taking a systems approach to my career development (as opposed to a single goal driven one), I am amassing a talent stack of differing, often unrelated, skills and fields of study. These stronger-than-average competencies in a large range of subjects will allow me to provide unique value in a way that not only pays the bills, but also makes a meaningful impact both inside and outside of Red Ventures.

My desire is to be an influential player online in the promotion of individual liberty. The greatest rush of adrenaline comes from transcribing a fact-based, well sourced, high-resolutioned, persuasive, original argument.

I am a podcast and non-fiction audiobook junkie in constant quest for new, golden nuggets of truth. My passion is the pursuit and application of wisdom, but my fulfillment lies with my love of family and God.
